# RB-005.5 Sensitive Data Impact Assessment

## Objective

Determine the sensitivity, regulatory impact, business exposure, and operational risk associated with exfiltrated or exposed data.

---

## Status

Live

---

## Inputs

- File inventory
- Data classification metadata
- DLP telemetry
- File access logs
- Business ownership information

---

## Tools

- DLP platform
- Data classification tooling
- SIEM
- Asset inventory
- Legal/compliance references

---

## Steps

### 1. Identify Impacted Data Sets

Determine:
- Files accessed
- Files transferred
- Database exposure
- SaaS data exposure
- Shared repository exposure

---

### 2. Assess Data Sensitivity

Classify:
- Public data
- Internal data
- Confidential data
- Restricted data
- Regulated data

---

### 3. Identify Regulatory Exposure

Review for:
- PII
- PCI data
- HIPAA/health records
- Financial records
- Government-regulated information

---

### 4. Assess Intellectual Property Exposure

Identify:
- Source code
- Trade secrets
- Internal documentation
- Product roadmaps
- Security architecture

---

### 5. Determine Business Impact

Assess:
- Operational impact
- Legal exposure
- Customer impact
- Reputational impact
- Financial exposure

---

### 6. Validate Exposure Scope

Determine:
- Confirmed exfiltration
- Suspected access only
- Public exposure
- Internal-only exposure

---

## Decision Points

| Condition | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Regulated data confirmed | Escalate to legal/compliance |
| Intellectual property exposed | Executive escalation |
| Public exposure identified | Immediate remediation |

---

## Output

- Impacted data inventory
- Sensitivity classification assessment
- Regulatory exposure assessment
- Business impact summary

---

## Common Failure Modes

- Treating all data equally
- Ignoring intellectual property exposure
- Failing to validate actual transfer success

---

## Automation Hooks

- Auto-data classification mapping
- Auto-regulatory tagging
