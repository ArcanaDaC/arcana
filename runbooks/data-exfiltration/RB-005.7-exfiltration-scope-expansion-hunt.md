# RB-005.7 Exfiltration Scope Expansion Hunt

## Objective

Identify additional systems, identities, datasets, or transfer activity related to a data exfiltration incident.

---

## Status

Live

---

## Inputs

- Transfer telemetry
- Authentication logs
- Endpoint telemetry
- DLP alerts
- Cloud audit logs
- IOC inventory

---

## Tools

- SIEM
- DLP platform
- EDR platform
- CASB platform
- Threat intelligence platform

---

## Steps

### 1. Hunt for Similar Transfers

Review:
- Similar transfer destinations
- Similar transfer timing
- Similar transfer methods
- Similar protocols
- Shared cloud storage usage

---

### 2. Identify Adjacent Systems

Assess:
- Shared endpoints
- Shared user devices
- Shared jump hosts
- Shared credentials
- Shared administrative access

---

### 3. Hunt for Historical Activity

Review:
- Previous transfer events
- Historical DLP alerts
- Historical archive creation
- Historical cloud uploads
- Historical sharing activity

---

### 4. Assess Identity Relationships

Identify:
- Shared accounts
- Shared sessions
- OAuth relationships
- Delegated access
- Privileged access overlap

---

### 5. Expand IOC Hunting

Search for:
- Known destinations
- Known hashes
- Known archive names
- Known transfer tooling
- Known attacker infrastructure

---

### 6. Reassess Scope

Determine:
- Total impacted systems
- Total impacted accounts
- Total exposed datasets
- Long-term exposure indicators

---

## Decision Points

| Condition | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Additional exposure identified | Expand incident scope |
| Historical exfiltration identified | Increase severity |
| Insider activity suspected | Escalate to PB-006 |

---

## Output

- Expanded scope assessment
- Additional impacted asset inventory
- Historical activity findings
- IOC expansion findings

---

## Common Failure Modes

- Focusing only on current activity
- Ignoring historical transfers
- Missing low-volume exfiltration patterns

---

## Automation Hooks

- Auto-historical telemetry correlation
- Auto-shared infrastructure mapping
- Auto-IOC expansion hunting
- Auto-transfer clustering
