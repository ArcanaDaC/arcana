# RB-005.8 Regulatory & Legal Coordination

## Objective

Coordinate legal review, regulatory assessment, stakeholder communication, and compliance obligations related to data exposure incidents.

---

## Status

Live

---

## Inputs

- Exposure assessment
- Data classification findings
- Regulatory mapping
- Business impact assessment
- Stakeholder inventory

---

## Tools

- Incident management platform
- Legal/compliance tracking systems
- Communication tooling
- Regulatory reference material

---

## Steps

### 1. Review Regulatory Exposure

Assess:
- Privacy obligations
- Industry-specific regulations
- Contractual obligations
- Customer notification requirements
- Jurisdictional impact

---

### 2. Engage Legal & Compliance Teams

Coordinate:
- Legal review
- Regulatory guidance
- Notification requirements
- Preservation requirements
- External counsel involvement

---

### 3. Establish Communication Cadence

Define:
- Stakeholder update frequency
- Executive briefing schedule
- External communication approval process
- Escalation pathways

---

### 4. Prepare Regulatory Documentation

Document:
- Incident timeline
- Exposure scope
- Impacted data
- Containment actions
- Recovery status

---

### 5. Coordinate Stakeholder Communications

Notify:
- Executive leadership
- Legal/compliance teams
- Business owners
- Regulators (if required)
- Customers/partners (if required)

---

### 6. Preserve Investigation Integrity

Ensure:
- Communication consistency
- Controlled disclosure
- Evidence preservation
- Approval workflows followed

---

## Decision Points

| Condition | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Regulated data confirmed | Regulatory escalation |
| Public exposure identified | Executive escalation |
| Contractual notification required | Immediate stakeholder engagement |

---

## Output

- Regulatory assessment
- Legal coordination timeline
- Stakeholder communication records
- Notification status

---

## Common Failure Modes

- Delayed legal escalation
- Inconsistent messaging
- Incomplete exposure documentation

---
