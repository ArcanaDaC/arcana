# RB-002.3 Encryption Scope Assessment

## Objective

Determine the full scope and business impact of ransomware encryption activity.

---

## Inputs

- EDR telemetry
- File server logs
- User reports
- SIEM alerts
- Network share telemetry

---

## Tools

- SIEM
- EDR platform
- File integrity monitoring
- Backup platform
- Asset inventory systems

---

## Steps

### 1. Identify Impacted Systems

Determine:
- Endpoints impacted
- Servers impacted
- Hypervisors impacted
- Cloud resources impacted

---

### 2. Identify Encrypted Data

Assess:
- File shares
- Databases
- User directories
- Critical applications
- SaaS platforms

---

### 3. Assess Business Impact

Determine:
- Business units impacted
- Critical services unavailable
- Operational disruption severity

---

### 4. Review Encryption Patterns

Identify:
- File extensions
- Ransom note patterns
- Encryption speed
- Selective targeting behaviour

---

### 5. Identify Backup Exposure

Determine:
- Backups encrypted
- Backup deletion activity
- Immutable storage impact

---

### 6. Build Impact Inventory

Document:
- Impacted systems
- Recovery priority
- Estimated restoration effort

---

## Decision Points

| Condition | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Critical infrastructure impacted | Escalate severity |
| Backup compromise identified | Immediate executive escalation |
| Cloud resources impacted | Expand scope investigation |

---

## Output

- Scope assessment
- Impact inventory
- Business impact summary
- Recovery prioritisation list

---

## Common Failure Modes

- Underestimating scope
- Ignoring cloud/SaaS impact
- Failing to identify backup compromise

---

## Automation Hooks

- Auto-identify impacted assets
- Auto-map impacted business services
- Auto-generate recovery inventory
