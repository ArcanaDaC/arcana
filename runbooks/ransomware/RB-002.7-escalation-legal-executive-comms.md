# RB-002.7 Escalation, Legal & Executive Communications

## Objective

Coordinate executive escalation, legal engagement, crisis communications, and regulatory response activities during a ransomware incident.

---

## Inputs

- Incident scope assessment
- Business impact assessment
- Legal/regulatory obligations
- Recovery status
- Executive stakeholder list

---

## Tools

- Incident management platform
- Crisis communication channels
- Legal/compliance tracking systems
- Executive briefing templates

---

## Steps

### 1. Notify Executive Leadership

Provide:
- Incident summary
- Business impact
- Current containment status
- Recovery estimates
- Key risks

---

### 2. Engage Legal & Compliance Teams

Assess:
- Regulatory notification obligations
- Data breach requirements
- Contractual obligations
- Cyber insurance requirements

---

### 3. Establish Crisis Communication Cadence

Define:
- Update frequency
- Stakeholder groups
- Communication approval process
- Escalation pathways

---

### 4. Coordinate Internal Communications

Notify:
- Leadership teams
- IT operations
- Security teams
- Business continuity teams

---

### 5. Prepare External Communication Strategy

Coordinate:
- Customer communications
- Media responses
- Regulatory engagement
- Third-party notifications

---

### 6. Document Executive Decisions

Track:
- Recovery approvals
- External communications approvals
- Legal guidance
- Risk acceptance decisions

---

## Decision Points

| Condition | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Data exfiltration confirmed | Breach notification review |
| Regulatory impact identified | Legal escalation |
| Critical outage ongoing | Crisis management escalation |

---

## Output

- Executive briefings
- Communication timeline
- Regulatory assessment
- Stakeholder communication records

---

## Common Failure Modes

- Delayed executive notification
- Inconsistent messaging
- Unapproved external communications

---

## Automation Hooks

- Auto-generate executive summaries
- Auto-create stakeholder distribution lists
- Auto-track escalation acknowledgements
